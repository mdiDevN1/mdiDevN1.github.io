Our History

MediaPark is an online store of household appliances and electronics, with delivery throughout the city of Tashkent and the entire Republic of Uzbekistan. Mediapark was founded in 2015 with the first store being open in Tashkent. Now MediaPark is the largest electronics store in the entire Republic of Uzbekistan, with stores being open in all large cities.
We offer the best and the most advanced devices on the market at competitive prices while providing people with the supperiour shopping experince.
